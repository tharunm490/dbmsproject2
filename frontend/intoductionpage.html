About the System

Our Crime Records Management System (CRMS) is a powerful digital platform designed to help police departments and law enforcement agencies manage crime-related data efficiently. It centralizes information related to criminal cases, suspects, victims, evidence, and case progress in a secure and structured manner. Built using modern technologies like Node.js, Express, MySQL, and styled with creative UI/UX principles, the system ensures ease of use, scalability, and security.
